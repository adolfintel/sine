Structure of a SINE Preset

A SINE preset consists in 1 background noise track and 1+ Entrainment tracks.
Each Entrainment track has a track volume and 3 envelopes:an Entrainment Frequency envelope, a Volume envelope and a Base Frequency envelope.
The Entrainment Frequency envelope regulates the frequency of the Isochronic pulses. This application supports frequencies from 0 to 40Hz.
The Volume envelope regulates the volume of the pulses (not to be confused with the track volume, which is constant)
The Base Frequency envelope regulates the frequency of the sine wave that is used to create the pulses.

Presets also have some information associated with: Title, Author and Description.
You can also make a part of a preset loop infinitely.
